ML in Depth 6 - Generative AI How to train your GAN

Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) have emerged as a powerful and transformative technology in the field of artificial intelligence. These fascinating neural network architectures have the ability to generate incredibly realistic and creative content, ranging from images and videos to music and text. However, taming these complex algorithms and training them effectively can be a daunting task for many AI enthusiasts and developers.

In this talk, we will delve into the world of GANs and explore the intricacies of training them. The session aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of the core concepts behind GANs, their architecture, and the training process. We will uncover the challenges faced during GAN training, such as mode collapse and instability, and discuss practical techniques to overcome these hurdles.
